Owner/Admin can log in and create New Users(employees) with a username and a password.
Users(employees) are able to clock in/clock out.
Users passwords are hashed when entered.
Users(employees) are able to enter sandwiches they made and keep track of time it took to make them.
Tracks employees that are currently clocked in.
Keeps track of employees with the lowest making time for sandwiches and are considered 'top' makers.
Keeps track of how long employees are on the clock for.
